Daily challenge

One challenge per calendar day (same for everyone on this device’s date). Coins and gems work the same as a normal run: your result is banked, ranked in your account history, and you earn the usual coin payout for a strong placement.

Quests & level

Levels are cosmetic only — show off your grind. XP per level uses whole numbers. Each run also grants placement XP from your live-board rank (up to 40 for 1st).

Complete a quest in a run, then claim the XP here — that slot then gets a new challenge for the rest of the day. Unclaimed rewards also show in the bar at the bottom of the screen.

Game mode
Easier difficulty pays fewer coins; Advanced and above earn the normal (100%) payout.

Progress (coins, gems, shop levels, run history) is stored locally and kept long-term. Old runs are only trimmed if you exceed a very large history cap. To remove this account entirely, use the button below.

Move: WASD or arrow keys (solo). Hold Space while moving to sprint (faster); sprinting costs 1 diamond per second. The view is zoomed in and follows you across a larger black arena. Eat cyan diamonds to grow and speed up. In 2 players (split screen), P1 is WASD + Space sprint; P2 is arrow keys + Right Shift sprint. If a teammate is eliminated, the survivor can revive them by meeting the killer (or the death spot if the killer despawned) with strictly more ◆ than the killer’s count (at the check, or frozen count if the killer is gone). In a bump, whoever has more diamonds is stronger. If your count is at least 10% higher than theirs (with whole numbers: your count × 10 ≥ their count × 11), they are eliminated and one-third of their diamonds spawn as pickups around where they died (anyone can collect them). If your lead is smaller than that, they only lose 10% of their diamonds (dropped near the hit). Accounts keep separate coins, gems, upgrades, and run history on this device; they are not deleted by the game except when you choose “Delete this account” in the menu (or if you clear browser site data). You can crush AI dots with the same rules; eliminated bots respawn elsewhere with 0 diamonds (their kill count is kept). The sidebar lists every dot ranked by diamonds and by kills. Menu while playing banks rewards like game over (current diamonds + kills). If you are crushed, the run ends; your diamonds when eliminated are your score for the saved leaderboard. Survival and Classical: coins depend on your place on the live leaderboard that round (top 10 pay 50→1 coins at full difficulty). Teams / Bed wars use team placement payouts. Saved classical boards still list runs by final ◆ for history only. Daily challenge follows the same mode rules. Your kills this run become gems added to your wallet. Spend coins and gems in the Shop on starting diamonds, speed, coin add-on, and magnet.
