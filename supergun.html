Supergun

16-dot arena · chests · guns · last dot wins



Higher difficulty = better aim, faster reactions, and smarter targeting (especially toward you).

Move with WASD or arrow keys. Left-click and hold to shoot toward the cursor. Right-click at the cursor to place a wall when a gun is selected, or to drink medicine when a potion is selected (blocks shots, 5 hits to break). You get 5 walls, then wait 5 seconds to recharge. Open brown chests for guns. Green medicine chests go into your inventory (Bottle +25, Carton +50, Big Glugger Pot +75). Select a potion and right-click to drink it. Right-click with a gun selected to build a wall. Scroll or press 1–5 to switch slots. Gun types: Rifle (normal), Minigun (stronger close), Sniper (stronger far). Type text shows only on dropped ground guns. Press Q to drop the gun in your selected slot (aim direction). Kills restore +50 HP. Walk over dropped guns and potions to pick them up (they stay on the ground if your inventory is full). Defeat 5 bosses for Legendary or Mythic guns. The ultra-rare Cat's Claw (Mythic, 90–130 dmg) only drops from bosses. Chests give Common through Epic. Be the last dot alive to win. The arena is huge — the view follows you; use the corner minimap to see the whole map. Accounts keep your name, color, difficulty, and win stats on this device across restarts.
