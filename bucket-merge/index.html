How to Play

🎯 Basic Gameplay

  • Drop Items: Click above the bucket to drop the displayed item
  • Merge Items: Same level items will automatically merge into higher levels
  • Score Points: Higher level items give more points
  • Earn Coins: Drop items to get coins, merge items for bonus coins

🎮 Game Modes

  • Memes Mode: Classic merge game with numbered items
  • Cats Mode: Merge cats from Shortleg Cat to NYAN CAT
  • Dogs Mode: Merge dogs from Chihuahua to German Shepherd

⚔️ Enemy System

  • Enemy Spawn: Enemies appear every 12-15 moves randomly
  • ❄️ Freeze Enemy: Freezes items for 5 drops (blue snowflake)
  • ⬇️ Level Down Enemy: Reduces item level by 1 (red arrow)
  • Frozen Items: Cannot merge while frozen

🛒 Shop System

  • Special Items: Extra Lives, Bucket Shake, Mega Merge
  • Backgrounds: Beach, City, Volcano, Matrix, Nebula
  • Trails: Fire, Ice, Electric, Star, Rainbow effects
  • Effects: Rainbow, Sparkle, Neon, Fire, Ice balls
  • Use Items: Press ENTER or number keys (1-9) to activate

🎵 Sound Effects

  • Cats Mode: Realistic "meow" sounds with pitch changes
  • Dogs Mode: Realistic "woof" sounds with pitch changes
  • Higher Levels: Lower pitch sounds

🏆 Special Rewards

  • Crown: Merge two level 12 items for a Crown (+500 coins)
  • Extra Lives: Save your game when bucket is full
  • Bucket Shake: Reorganize items with SPACEBAR
